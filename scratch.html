What Makes Linear Algebra Interesting?

Math can seem incredibly drab and boring at times, especially when its uses are unclear. However, I think that linear algebra
is able to avoid this problem due to it being used in a so many diverse ways. From it being used in computers all the time through
search engine results or programs that have the abilities to zoom in to it being the basis for most or even all of statistics, linear
algebra really is everywhere.

Of course, it can be easy to lose interest in something when it doesn't make much sense, and in linear algebra that isn't
an uncommo occurance. However, there are many great resources online that can help the learning process. One in particular is the playlist
by 3Blue1Brown.
